ITALY

Italy is the country on which reside the ruins of the former great Roman Empire.

The country is located in the Italic Peninsula, in the southern region of Europe.

HISTORY

Italy was the birth place of one of the greatest empires in humanity's history, the Roman Empire. Since then, they never managed to reach the same power, but they became a huge cultural point of influence throughout Europe.
