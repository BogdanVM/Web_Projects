ENGLAND

England is one of the superpowers of the Colonialism Era.

The country is located in the Britanic Peninsula, in the northwestern region of Europe. Their language is spoken all around the world.

HISTORY

England used to be a great colonial empire. They used to rule over the entire island of Britania and, at the peak of their nation, over 24% of the Earth's total land area. Since then, they lost their colonies but have maintained their status as one of the world's superpowers.
